About

BollywoodGuess.io is a free online Multiplayer Movie Guessing game

A normal game consists of few rounds, where every round a player has to select desired movie name, its actor, actress and songs and others need to guess it to gain points.

The person with the most points at the end of the game, will be then crowned as the winner!
